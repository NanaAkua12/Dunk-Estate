Dunkeld Accommodation

A short drive outside of the quaint historical town Dullstroom, Dunkeld Country & Equestrian Estate is the ideal accommodation to explore the highest village South Africa or spend time with your passions. When staying with us you can choose between the rustic charm of the Country Estate with gorgeous views of trees celebrating the season and the trout filled dams, or the Equestrian Estate with endless grasslands and manicured gardens surrounding the accommodation and stables.

Our accommodation ranges from 4 star hotel rooms to self-catering villas and chalets that can accommodate couples, large families, businessmen and everyone in between. Dunkeld Country & Equestrian Estate truly is the perfect accommodation in Dullstroom, whether you're looking for a well-deserved family vacation, a romantic weekend away or a fishing trip.

In order to provide our wildlife/fish reserves with the best possible natural surroundings we charge a Conservation Fee of R180 per vehicle for the duration of our guests' stay.
